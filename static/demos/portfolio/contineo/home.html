Who are Contineo?

Contineo has been established since 1998, expanding and becoming incorporated in 2002. We are a small independent company that focuses on the network essentials required by companies. We specialise in bespoke solutions to a small to medium business, including members of the public and local schools.

Our friendly staff has a wide range of hardware and software knowledge and are more than happy to help with any problems small or large. We also work with colleges and schools within the community giving students the opportunity to gain more hands on knowledge and work experience within the IT industry.

We offer services that include:
  • Web Hosting
  • Dedicated Online Servers
  • Bespoke Network Solutions

We are happy to consult on any scale project, giving a reasonable quotation on projects and a professional finish to the job. Please us the Contact Us section of the website for all enquiries.
