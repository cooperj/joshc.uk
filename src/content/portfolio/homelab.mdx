My homelab is an ongoing project where I run and maintain a collection of self-hosted services and infrastructure at home. It covers networking, storage, virtualisation, and automation, and it is always evolving.

Currently I am running mostly UniFi networking equipment, a Proxmox hypervisor for virtualisation, and a variety of self-hosted applications for personal use. I also have a few Raspberry Pi devices running various services.

I am currently in the process of migrating to Kubernetes for container orchestration, which will allow me to manage my services more efficiently and scale them as needed and work in a more enterprise way. This also allows me to go further with automation and monitoring, which is something I am very interested in.
